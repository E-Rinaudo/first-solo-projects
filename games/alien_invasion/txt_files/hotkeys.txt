                    GAMEPLAY KEYS

● PLAY/RESUME/RESTART ---> ENTER

● QUIT GAME ---> ESC

● PAUSE ---> P

● MENU ---> M

                    DIFFICULTY KEYS

● DIFFICULTY ---> D

● EASY ---> 1

● MEDIUM ---> 2 (DEFAULT DIFFICULTY IF NONE SELECTED)

● HARD ---> 3

                    SHIP KEYS

● MOVE SHIP RIGHT ---> RIGHT ARROW

● MOVE SHIP LEFT ---> LEFT ARROW

● SHOOT ---> SPACEBAR 

                    ADDITIONAL KEYS

● HOTKEYS ---> K

● CREDITS ---> C

● CLOSE HOTKEYS and CREDITS ---> E
